    CNET editors' review

    Reviewed by: CNET Staff

    Experience the world directly from your kitchen with this recipe program that offers several international types of cuisine. Every-Occasion International Recipes' interface lacks spice but is blissfully easy to understand. Learn how to prepare culinary delights from 16 countries and areas, including Latin America, Indonesia, and Greece. Recipes are sorted by country not by type of food, and the search feature isn't available in the trial version, which may make it a bit more difficult to find a specific recipe. Although it has an option to add recipes, this feature and the option to print recipes aren't available in the trial version either. Any cooking enthusiast looking for new dishes will find plenty of recipes with easy to understand directions.

    Publisher's description

    From SoftCircuits :

    Contains a library of 310 recipes from 16 countries--from Abalone to Zabione. Convenient search feature locates specific recipes, or lists all those recipes with a particular ingredient. You can add your own countries and recipes and there is even a feature to enter the names of friends and business associates and a list of their favorite recipes. Print recipes to any Windows-compatible printer.
