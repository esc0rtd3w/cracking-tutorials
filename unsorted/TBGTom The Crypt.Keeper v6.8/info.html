    Publisher's description

    From tbgtom.com :

    A simple utility to keep track of, and optionally map out, your cemetery records. Most of the cemeteries I have worked with have nothing more than a very old thick book that they continue to write new entries into. If anything were to happen to that book all the records would be lost forever. Now is the time to move that information to media that can be backed-up and saved in multiple places in case of accidental data loss.
