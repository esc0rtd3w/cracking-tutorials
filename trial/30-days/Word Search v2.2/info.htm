How to download word lists
The (download all) links are zip files and require an un-zipping utility.
The individuual word lists are *.wds files. "wordsearch" and can be saved and used as is.
Just left-click the zip/individual file you want and select "Save".
This will bring up a "Save As" dialog box on your computer allowing you to save it
wherever you want on your computer.

FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions
Q: Will you be adding font control in a future version?
A: I haven't decided yet. I wanted to create a "simple" wordsearch creation program.
So I am attempting to keep it simple. The size of the font is determined by the number
of words in the puzzle. The smaller the number of words, the larger the font. Exception: The
size of the puzzle will be at least as large as the largest word.
